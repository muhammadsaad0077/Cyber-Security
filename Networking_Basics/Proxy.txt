Proxy:

It is a server/computer/software in the middle that monitor the traffic and then decide whether to format this packet to the destination or not


Alice (send facebook.com)  -----   Proxy (it checks facebook.com is not allowed in my list so delete/drop that request)    ----- Bob


Proxy:
Advantages:

Faster, less overhead.

Easy setup, cost-effective.
Use Cases:

Bypass geo-restrictions.

Low-latency tasks.


VPN:
Advantages:

Comprehensive security.

Overall privacy.
Considerations:

Slower due to encryption.

More config, subscription cost.

Choose Based On:

Speed: Proxy for quick tasks, VPN for security.

Use Cases: Proxy for specific, VPN for general.

Ease: Proxy for simplicity, VPN for coverage.

Cost: Proxy for budget, VPN for features.

Compatibility: Proxy for apps, VPN for all.
